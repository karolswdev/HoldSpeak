# The Desk

The Desk (`/desk`) is HoldSpeak's spatial home for everything the Primitive
Framework knows about: your meetings, notes, knowledge bases, agents, chains,
workflows, and directories. Instead of a list, it is a warm 2.5D world where
each primitive is a hand-drawn object you can arrange, file, and open. It reads
the same live data as the rest of the app, from your local hub.

## What you see

Every primitive floats on the desk as a pixel-art object, with a soft shadow
and a gentle bob:

- **meetings** are cassette tapes,
- **notes** are notepads,
- **knowledge bases** are crystals,
- **agents** and **coder sessions** are little characters,
- **chains** and **workflows** are cartridges.

A freshly created object arrives with a short glow so you can spot it.

## Arrange the desk

Drag any object to move it. The desk remembers where you put things on this
device (the layout is stored locally in your browser and never leaves your
machine). Press **Tidy** in the header to snap everything back to the automatic
layout.

## Directories are shelves

A directory shows up as a painted shelf-zone. Two things you can do with it:

- **File**: drag an object onto a shelf to file it there.
- **Dive**: click a shelf to go inside and see only what is filed in it. Use
  **All primitives** to come back out.

## Open something

Click an object to open it. A meeting opens its archive entry; the other kinds
reveal their full card, with the actions you would expect (run an agent, file
into a directory, and so on).

## Where it lives

The two modes (Dictation and Meetings) stay the front door. The Desk lives in
the **Studio** tier, and Home carries an **Enter the Desk** entry so the world
is one click away when you want it.

## Qlippy

If you turn the mascot on in **Settings**, Qlippy keeps you company in the
corner of the desk. It is off by default and purely for company.
