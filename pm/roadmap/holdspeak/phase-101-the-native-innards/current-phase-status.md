## Why this phase exists

Phase 100 fixed the SHAPE of every interior — what leads, what folds
behind wings and doors, what dies. The bodies themselves still
COMPOSE as generic kit: label-over-input stacks, uniform SurfaceRows,
one text size, verbs in action rows. docs/internal/AGENT_BRIEF.md §5
carries the diagnosis and the six commitments: an interior type
scale; data as material (edit in place, kill the form stacks); purpose-
built compositions (the journal reads like a journal, Blocks like a
library, Runs on like a switchboard, the transcript like a script);
verbs on the material; direct manipulation reaching INTO windows;
motion as meaning.
