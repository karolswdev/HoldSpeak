## What shipped

- SQLite v23 (additive): `work_attempts` with opaque never-reused ids,
  compound identity (node/source/worktree/story/session/target), CHECKed
  provenance kinds and states, and a partial UNIQUE index that makes it
  impossible for one session to hold two live exact attempts;
  `work_attempt_events` appends every transition with reason classifiers.
  Schema snapshot regenerated by the documented flow.
- State machine with sticky terminal states, quiet same-state no-ops, and
  honest resilience: worktree removal → abandoned with history, node
  offline → unknown (recoverable), stale timeout sweep.
- Creation paths: manual (provenance forced server-side), rider_claim
  (hooks detect typed story refs or env claims; resolution goes through the
  Delivery Source registry before anything is marked exact — unresolvable
  claims are skipped, never guessed), and the dw-sessions heuristic kept as
  labeled-ambiguous capped rows that can never shadow or downgrade an exact
  attempt.
- Wire: `attempts_schema:1`, path-free, with explicit association and
  history.
