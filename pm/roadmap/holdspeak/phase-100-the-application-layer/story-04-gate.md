# HS-100-04 — The owner's gate

- **Project:** holdspeak
- **Phase:** 100
- **Status:** backlog
- **Depends on:** HS-100-03
- **Unblocks:** HS-100-05

## Problem

Every prior UI phase shipped to a machine-verifiable bar and failed
the owner's. This phase makes the owner's verdict the gate itself.

## Scope

- In:
  - the grounding + judgment + thesis + mockups presented for the
    owner's sitting (artifact page + live mockups);
  - revision rounds until the owner calls the direction — the story
    stays open through as many rounds as it takes;
  - the verdict recorded verbatim; the build stories (HS-100-05)
    written/adjusted to the approved thesis at close.
- Out:
  - building past the mockups before the call.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] The owner has explicitly approved the thesis (recorded).
- [ ] The build stories reflect the approved version, not the draft.

## Test plan

- n/a — this gate is human by design.

## Evidence required

- The verdict, verbatim; the revision log.
