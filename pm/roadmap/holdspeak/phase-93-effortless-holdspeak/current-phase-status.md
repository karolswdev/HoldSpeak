## Goal

Turn HoldSpeak's shipped convergence substrate into a visibly simpler and more
dependable AI operating system centered on the Desk: remove competing entry
points and ambiguous language, express rich capability through coherent OS
affordances and progressive mastery, retain work through real failures, and
prove the daily experience through sustained owner use on Web, iPhone, and iPad.

## Scope

### In

- Visible subtraction in Web and native arrival, navigation, creation, and
  advanced-tool discovery.
- A shared Desk OS-affordance grammar for selection, contextual actions,
  inspectors, windows/workrooms, tool shelf/dock, search/launcher, live process
  presence, attention, receipts, keyboard commands, drag/drop, and recovery.
- Context-bearing entry to Dictation, Meetings, Workbench, Integration setup,
  and other focused rooms, with retained result and return to a Desk subject.
- Final cross-client product-language and consequential-verb remediation.
- A complete professional-copy pass across React, flagship Swift, CLI/doctor,
  UAT, and user documentation: concise operational language with marketing,
  storytelling, anthropomorphic filler, and redundant explanation removed.
- First-class Desk presence for Projects, Integrations, capabilities,
  destinations, devices, live processes, control mode, attention, and receipts;
  Studio remains their focused authoring/configuration room, not their primary
  home.
- One shared Secure/Normal/YOLO automation-posture contract across the hub,
  React, and flagship Swift: YOLO has zero HoldSpeak approval prompts for
  eligible configured/registered actions, while every posture preserves hard
  prerequisites, invariants, and receipts.
- Real microphone dictation and meeting-capture resilience, offline sync,
  conflict recovery, and failure retention.
- Semantic/non-drag Web and native paths, VoiceOver, keyboard, Dynamic Type,
  Reduce Motion, compact layouts, and large Desk datasets.
- Short before/after owner observations in every UI story and a sustained
  multi-day dogfood close.
- Verification-rail repairs required to make the product and UAT suites bounded,
  repeatable, and honest.

### Out

- A new home, dashboard, control center, universal queue, or chat-first shell.
- Turning Studio into the real advanced product while leaving the Desk as a
  simplified launcher.
- New product capability unrelated to an existing primary journey.
- A universal entity/event-store or workflow-engine rewrite.
- Deleting expert capability merely to lower a control count.
- Removing stable deep links or CLI/deployment surfaces.
- Pixel-identical Web and Swift UI.
- A marketplace, Windows client, fleet administration, or third-party sandbox.

- [ ] Keyboard-only Web and physical iPhone/iPad VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Reduce

- [ ] Canonical Python lanes, `npm run check`, UAT Web tests, Swift package and

- [ ] Five working days of owner dogfood produce no lost work, false completion,

      attached.
- [ ] Phase 92 is not marked complete by implication: any Phase-92 criterion

## Where we are

HS-93-01 started the remediation with measured visible subtraction. HS-93-02
now makes focused work visibly belong to the Desk: one versioned identity-only
context is shared by Web, Hub, and Swift; contextual rooms name their subject
and return; direct routes use an explicit Desk fallback; and Workbench/live
Meeting retain draft or result identity outside the URL. A real Hub serving the
production Web bundle now proves ordinary-click contextual entry, direct
fallback, compact layout, and zero failed API responses; flagship simulator
captures cover the native sheet state. Both stories remain open because these
bounded captures do not satisfy owner, every-room completion/failure,
keyboard-only, or physical-device gates.

HS-93-03 now gives the product one versioned vocabulary and a broad regression
census across the full React client, full flagship Swift app, CLI, current user
guides, and authority UAT. Secure/Normal/YOLO render above compatible
`safe`/`neutral`/`yolo` wires; Persona, Coder session, Runs on, destination
classes, lifecycle state, content review, and effect approval are distinct. The
3,919-candidate census is green with zero violations and only exact, reasoned
exceptions. The story remains open for owner and physical-device read-through
and complete forced-failure copy evidence.

HS-93-04 now composes Projects, configured Integrations, ready capabilities,
Runs-on targets, Coder presence, devices, and background work through the
existing Desk Tools/search, selection, inspector, workroom, attention, and
Receipt primitives. The Web's permanent Persona/model rail is removed and the
background conveyor rests as a non-overlapping presence chip. Integration
proposals carry a live qualified source through authority and project their
Receipt back to that subject; flagship Swift shares the request wire and adds a
direct Project inspector without matching Web chrome. API-backed production Web
captures pass the complete Slack approval/source-return path on desktop and
compact layouts. The story remains open for owner discovery measures and
physical iPhone/iPad completion/failure/relaunch evidence.

HS-93-05 now measures first value from content-free observed events and server
time rather than trusted client counters. Web editors persist synchronously;
Web and flagship Apple Dictate checkpoint released audio before transcription;
and paired delivery carries one durable request identity from the sending
device through a hub claim and terminal Receipt. A reconnect reads the cached
Receipt, a changed payload is refused, and an indeterminate hook remains pending
instead of replaying. Default Web workers are bounded and real-microphone/model
tests are explicit opt-ins. Production Web evidence proves relaunch retention,
factual timeout actions, event-derived counters, and one-effect reconnect with
synthetic content. The story remains open for the complete real fault matrix,
active-capture interruption, alternate actions, HS-93-07 control modes, owner
observation, and physical Web/iPhone/iPad evidence.

HS-93-06 has started with the first owner-visible conflict-recovery slice.
Equal-clock Meeting divergence was already retained by the hub but had no write
path or client decision surface. History and the Meeting's Desk pull-out now
show both exact versions and require an explicit Keep-current, Use-synced, or
named destructive deletion choice. Incoming content replaces the same Meeting
identity and resolves atomically; malformed or substituted content refuses with
both versions retained. The story remains open for production captures, native
parity, long-duration memory/checkpoint traces, the complete fault matrix,
partial-intelligence Retry/Skip, airplane-mode exactly-once sync, and owner and
physical-device evidence.

Phase 91 remains the roadmap's current phase and its owner/Swift close gate is
non-waivable. Phase 92 still has broad automated implementation but no accepted
user-experience close. Phase 93 closes only through real Web and physical-device
use; no final summary exists.

## Decisions made (this phase)

- 2026-07-11 — Phase 93 is a remediation phase, not a feature phase — direct
  owner clarification that the real goal is streamlined, robust, easier use.
- 2026-07-11 — Preserve Phase-92 substrate but do not equate it with the user
  outcome — implementation-versus-experience audit.
- 2026-07-11 — The Desk remains the primary architecture; remediation removes
  peers and ambiguity rather than inventing a replacement shell — owner canon.
- 2026-07-11 — The Desk is HoldSpeak's AI operating system, not a launcher;
  Studio is a focused authoring/configuration workroom and advanced capability
  must remain discoverable and operable through Desk OS affordances — direct
  owner clarification.
- 2026-07-11 — Product posture is Secure/Normal/YOLO with compatibility for the
  current `safe`/`neutral` wire values; YOLO means zero HoldSpeak approval
  prompts for eligible configured/registered actions, not weakened correctness
  invariants — direct owner clarification.
- 2026-07-11 — Product copy is professional and streamlined; visual personality
  may remain, but marketing slop, storytelling, lore, faux drama,
  anthropomorphic filler, and redundant prose do not belong in operational UI —
  direct owner clarification.
- 2026-07-11 — Owner observation occurs throughout implementation, with a
  sustained dogfood close — the absence of lived evidence is itself the current
  gap.
- 2026-07-11 — Focused rooms carry a versioned identity-and-orientation contract,
  never authored content; unknown future metadata is tolerated, unsafe return
  paths are refused, and direct entry does not fabricate a Desk subject —
  HS-93-02 implementation contract.
- 2026-07-11 — Product language registry v2 separates Secure/Normal/YOLO labels
  from compatible wires and makes full React, flagship Swift, CLI, guide, and
  UAT copy a classified, failing census with exact reasoned exceptions —
  HS-93-03 implementation contract.
- 2026-07-11 — Contextual power is an adapter over qualified selection,
  capability readiness/effects, trust destinations, Projects, Runs-on targets,
  live processes, and existing Receipts; no new engine or persisted noun is
  introduced. External-effect source identity is authority-bound and projected
  back to the originating Desk subject — HS-93-04 implementation contract.
- 2026-07-11 — Dictation recovery is a sequence of local checkpoints: content-
  free observed first-value events, device-local audio before transcription,
  device-local editable text plus a stable delivery id, and a hub claim/Receipt
  before the desktop effect. An indeterminate effect remains pending and is
  never replayed automatically — HS-93-05 implementation contract.
- 2026-07-11 — A Meeting sync conflict stays unresolved until the owner chooses
  the current or incoming version at the Meeting subject. Applying incoming
  content and resolving the retained conflict is one transaction; deletion is
  named explicitly, and no keep-both claim exists without creating another
  object — HS-93-06 conflict-recovery contract.
- 2026-07-11 — Phase 91 remains current and the roadmap pointer does not move —
  Delivery Workbench live state.
